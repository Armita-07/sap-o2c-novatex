# NovaTex Industries Pvt. Ltd. — Company Blueprint

## Company Overview
NovaTex Industries is a fictitious mid-sized textile company used for this SAP capstone project. It manufactures and distributes cotton and synthetic fabrics across eastern India.

## Organizational Structure

### Financial Accounting (FI)
- Company Code: NTX1
- Chart of Accounts: INT (International)
- Fiscal Year: K4 (April–March)
- Currency: INR

### Sales & Distribution (SD)
- Sales Org: NTX_SO
- Distribution Channels: 10 (Wholesale), 20 (Retail)
- Divisions: 01 (Textiles), 02 (Accessories)
- Sales Areas: NTX_SO/10/01 (Primary)

### Materials Management (MM)
- Plant: NTX_P1 (Bhubaneswar Manufacturing)
- Storage Location: 0001 (Finished Goods)
- Purchasing Org: NTX_PO

## Key Business Scenarios
1. O2C: Domestic wholesale orders from fabric distributors
2. Pricing: Standard price list + GST + freight surcharges
3. Payment Terms: Net 30 days for wholesale customers

## Master Data Summary
- 3 Customers configured (Wholesale segment)
- 5 Materials (Cotton Fabric variants)
- Pricing conditions: PR00, MWST, KF00 (Freight)
